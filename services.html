Wellness

Head Spa &
Scalp Treatments

Treatments that address various hair and scalp concerns. Each is tailored to your specific concern — using argan oil, amino acid, and aloe-infused formulas to restore balance from root to tip.

Each Head Spa treatment begins with a thorough scalp analysis and is tailored to your hair and scalp type. We address the root cause of your concern — not just the symptoms.

Thinning Hair / Hair Loss
Scalp Detox — removes build-up to improve scalp health and encourage better growth. Hair Growth Stimulation Therapy — gentle massage using oils to stimulate blood circulation. Micronutrient Infusion — treatments infused with amino acids to strengthen thinning hair.
Dandruff / Itchy Scalp
Hydrating Scalp Masks with aloe vera to hydrate and soothe itchiness. Exfoliating Scalp Treatment to remove dead skin cells and dandruff for a clean, balanced scalp.
Dry Hair
Deep Conditioning Treatment enriched with argan oil to restore moisture. Hot Oil Treatment — a blend of oils to nourish and seal the hair shaft. Steam Treatment — opens the hair cuticle to allow intensive hydration to penetrate deeply.

Volume & Length

Tape Hair
Extensions

A semi-permanent, lightweight, and virtually invisible method to add length, volume, and thickness to your hair — with no compromise on quality or comfort.

Tape hair extensions are made with high-quality, natural-looking hair and discreetly applied using medical-grade adhesive that lies flat against your scalp, ensuring a seamless blend with your natural hair. They move exactly like your own hair — no one will know.

  • Medical-grade adhesive — gentle on your natural hair
  • Available in a full range of shades and lengths
  • Can be worn up, down, or in any style
  • Reusable and repositionable at maintenance visits
  • Lasts 6–8 weeks between maintenance appointments
