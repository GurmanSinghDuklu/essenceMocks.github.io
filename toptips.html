Before Your Appointment

Do I need a patch test?

Yes — a patch test and consultation is required if any of the following apply to you:

  • You are a new client booking any colour service
  • You have had a change in any medication
  • It has been over 12 months since your last appointment
Please note: Patch tests and consultations take no more than 5–10 minutes and must be pre-booked. They cannot be carried out on the day of your appointment.
Booking

How to book an appointment

Appointments can be booked via our online booking system. For any questions or enquiries, contact us on 07375 956 131. Please allow 48–72 hours for a reply.

  • For a specific date, please allow at least 4 weeks' notice

Preparation

How to prepare for your appointment

  • Wear comfortable clothes you don't mind getting stained. Avoid hooded tops.
  • Bring inspiration photos — the more specific, the better.
  • Ensure your hair is freshly washed for all colour services.
Aftercare

Looking after your results

The care you give your hair at home directly affects how long your results last. Follow these guidelines to protect your investment and keep your hair looking its best.

🎨
Hair Colour Aftercare
  • Wait at least 48 hours before washing after a colour treatment
  • Use sulphate-free shampoos designed for colour-treated hair
  • Rinse with lukewarm or cool water to preserve colour
  • Use a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week
  • Always apply heat protection before styling
  • Book regular trims and toner appointments to keep colour fresh
  • Use UV-protective products in prolonged sun exposure
✂️
Haircut & Style Aftercare
  • Schedule regular trims every 6–8 weeks to maintain shape
  • Always use a heat protectant before styling tools
  • Detangle gently with a wide-tooth comb — start from the tips up
  • Use a nourishing conditioner or leave-in conditioner regularly
✨
Hair Treatment Aftercare
  • Use sulphate-free shampoos and conditioners
  • Keratin only: Do not wash for 72 hours after treatment
  • Avoid tight hairstyles (buns, ponytails) for the first few days
  • No swimming or excessive sweating for the first 72 hours
  • Avoid moisture and humidity in the first 72 hours post-keratin
