Booking & Appointments

How can I book an appointment?
+
Appointments can be booked via our online booking system. For questions or enquiries, contact us on 07375 956 131. Please allow 48–72 hours for a reply.

If you are looking for a specific date, please allow at least 4 weeks' notice, and 6 weeks for a Saturday appointment. Appointments are only confirmed once a deposit has been paid.
Do I need to pay a deposit?
+
Yes — appointments are only confirmed once a deposit has been paid. This secures your booking and helps us manage our diary effectively. The deposit will be deducted from the total cost of your service.
How far in advance should I book?
+
For a specific date, please allow at least 4 weeks' notice. Saturday appointments require a minimum of 6 weeks' notice. We recommend booking as early as possible to avoid disappointment, especially around school holidays and peak periods.
What is your cancellation policy?
+
Please give as much notice as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in your deposit being retained. We appreciate your understanding — last-minute gaps are difficult to fill in a small business.

Colour Services

Do I need a patch test before a colour service?
+
Yes — a patch test and consultation is required if:
  • You are a new client booking any colour service
  • You have had a change in any medication
  • It has been over 12 months since your last appointment
Patch tests and consultations take no more than 5–10 minutes and must be pre-booked. They cannot be done on the day of your appointment.
What is balayage and is it right for me?
+
Balayage is a freehand colouring technique that creates a natural, sun-kissed look with soft blended highlights. It's ideal for low-maintenance colour that grows out beautifully without harsh regrowth lines. It works on almost all hair types and lengths. We'll advise during your consultation whether it's the right technique for your hair and goals.
How should I prepare my hair for a colour service?
+
Please ensure your hair is freshly washed on the day of your colour appointment. Bring inspiration photos if you have them — the more specific, the better. Wear comfortable clothing you don't mind getting stained, and avoid hooded tops.
How long does colour last?
+
This varies depending on the technique, your hair type, and how you care for it at home. Balayage typically needs refreshing every 3–5 months. Regular toner appointments every 6–8 weeks will keep the tone looking fresh and vibrant. We'll give you personalised aftercare advice after every colour service.

Hair Treatments

What is the difference between Nanoplastia and Keratin?
+
Both are smoothing treatments but they work differently. Keratin replenishes your hair's natural protein, creating a smooth, frizz-free finish that typically lasts 3–5 months. Nanoplastia works at a molecular level to restructure and hydrate the hair — it's gentler, organic, and doesn't fully eliminate curl; it refines it. Both are 100% formaldehyde-free. We'll advise which is better suited for your hair type during consultation.
Are the treatments safe? Do they contain formaldehyde?
+
All of our treatments — Keratin, Nanoplastia, and Hair Botox — are 100% organic and completely formaldehyde-free. We only use professional-grade, safe formulations that deliver exceptional results without compromising your health or hair integrity.
How long do smoothing treatments last?
+
Results typically last 3–5 months depending on your hair type, how often you wash your hair, and the products you use at home. Using sulphate-free shampoos and conditioners significantly extends the life of all smoothing treatments.
Can I have a smoothing treatment on coloured hair?
+
Yes — our organic treatments are safe for colour-treated hair. We recommend having colour done before your smoothing treatment, not after. We'll always assess your hair's condition before proceeding and advise on the best approach for your individual hair.

Hair Extensions

What types of extensions do you offer?
+
We offer tape hair extensions as our client-facing service — a semi-permanent, lightweight method that lies flat against the scalp and blends seamlessly with natural hair. They're held in place with medical-grade adhesive and last 6–8 weeks before requiring a maintenance appointment.
Will extensions damage my natural hair?
+
When applied correctly and maintained properly, tape extensions cause no damage to natural hair. We use medical-grade adhesive that is gentle on the hair shaft. We'll always assess your natural hair condition before fitting and advise on the best maintenance routine to keep your hair healthy.
